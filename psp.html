Sony PlayStation Portable 2004

Sony's flagship handheld, built with the goal of being "essentially a portable PS2". A 4.3" 16:9 widescreen TFT LCD (480×272), MIPS R4000 CPU, the proprietary UMD optical disc format, and 802.11b Wi-Fi multiplayer brought a near-console experience into your pocket. Its piano-black gloss body and metallic-finish buttons defined Sony's signature design language.

CPUMIPS R4000 @ 333 MHz
Display480×272 TFT LCD, 4.3"
MediaUMD (Universal Media Disc)
BatteryBuilt-in lithium-ion, ~6 hours
Weight280 g
MaterialPiano-black gloss ABS
