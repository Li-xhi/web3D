Nintendo Game Boy Advance SP 2003

The most significant revision of the GB line. Its clamshell shell protects the screen, a frontlit LED display finally solves the dim-screen problem of the original GBA, and a built-in lithium battery (~3 hours to charge, ~18 hours of play) replaces AA cells. Players still praise its painted metal finish and hinge as "the most durable handheld design ever made".

CPUARM7TDMI @ 16.78 MHz
Display240×160 TFT LCD (frontlit)
BatteryBuilt-in lithium-ion, ~18 hours
Weight143 g (closed)
MaterialMetallic-painted ABS clamshell
Launch price¥12,500 (Japan)

Interaction Guide

  • Drag to rotate the model
  • Scroll wheel to zoom in/out
  • Flip — play the lid open/close animation (forwards = close, again = re-open)
  • Closed / Open camera presets also drive the flip animation
  • Wireframe — inspect the underlying mesh
  • Light — toggle the main directional light
  • Rotate — auto-rotate the camera around the model
  • Color — tint the model; Reset restores the original colours
  • Use the top bar to play / pause / skip / adjust the museum BGM
